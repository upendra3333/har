Tribute to MS Dhoni

MS Dhoni is a former Indian cricketer and captain. He is considered one of the greatest captains in the history of cricket. Dhoni is known for his calm demeanor, sharp cricketing mind, and the ability to finish matches with his explosive batting.

Under his captaincy, India achieved many milestones including winning the ICC T20 World Cup in 2007, ICC Cricket World Cup in 2011, and ICC Champions Trophy in 2013. Dhoni is also known for his leadership qualities and is often referred to as "Captain Cool".

After retiring from international cricket, Dhoni continues to play in the Indian Premier League (IPL) and is admired by millions of fans around the world.
